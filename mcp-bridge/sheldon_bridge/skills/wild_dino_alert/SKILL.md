# Wild Dino Alert

Monitors for dangerous wild dinosaurs nearby and alerts the tribe. Automatically flags high-level predators (Giganotosaurus, Rex, Spino, etc.) within alert range. Triggers a notification to the player who discovered it and can auto-slay if configured.

## Triggers
wild dino, alert, danger dino, 危险恐龙,野生龙

## Examples
"check for wild dinos nearby"
"is there a giga near my base?"
"alert me to dangerous wild dinos"
"scan for predators"

## Auto-Trigger-On
wild_dino_alert
