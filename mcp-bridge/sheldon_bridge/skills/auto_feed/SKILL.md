# Auto Feed

Automatically feeds all dinosaurs belonging to the player's tribe when triggered. Useful after a baby dino is born or during raid defense.

## Triggers
feed, auto-feed,喂食,feed tribe,feed dinos

## Examples
"feed all my tribe's dinos"
"auto-feed the baby giga that was just born"
"feed tribe"

## Auto-Trigger-On
baby_born
dino_tamed

## Tier
vip
